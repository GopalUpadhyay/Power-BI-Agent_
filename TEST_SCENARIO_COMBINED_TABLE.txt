NEW COMBINED TABLE: SalesPerformanceAnalysis
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Purpose: Multi-dimensional sales analytics combining:
  ✓ Sales individuals (SalesPerson)
  ✓ Their sales transactions (Sales)
  ✓ Sale targets (Targets)
  ✓ Product details (Product)
  ✓ Regional information (Region)

Use Case: Executive dashboard to track:
  - Individual salesperson performance vs targets
  - Product-wise sales by region
  - Target achievement rates
  - Sales rep productivity metrics
